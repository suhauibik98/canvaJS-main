Training and Consulting

Tajseer provides distance learning and training services, virtual reality, STEM methodology, and training /educational consultancy. It has been working to meet the needs of real and virtual e-learning solutions, starting in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia since the year (2009), and most of its attention is focused on meeting the growing needs of educational institutions and companies in the field of Elearning and educational solutions. Our team includes a group of academic experts from international universities from various fields in addition to professional consultants to ensure keeping pace with the latest technical and educational developments. The team of certified trainers provides real and virtual training within the specialized training programs. Consulting and training techniques are periodically updated to ensure the application of international best practices and the transformation of traditional educational patterns into smart technological means.
